Our Objectives

We are passionate about young girls who are rape victims and those who are not. To create awareness about the effects of rape and self defense skills needed to protect themselves from being raped. To also make the rape victims realize that they are not alone and can be successful and will also like to link them with successful female celebrities who were once a rape victim. This is to make them know that being raped isn't the end of the world
